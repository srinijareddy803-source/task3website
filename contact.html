Frequently Asked Questions

Before sending an inquiry, check our FAQ section for quick answers regarding our programs.

What are the admissions prerequisites?

Our beginner-friendly courses like Web Development and UI/UX design require no prior background knowledge. However, advanced tracks like AI & Machine Learning require comfortable python coding literacy and basic linear algebra concepts.

Can I complete these cohorts on a part-time basis?

Yes. Most cohorts feature lectures and discussions during evening sessions (starting around 6:00 PM EST) to accommodate students who work full-time or are currently enrolled in high school/college classes.

Do you offer career placement resources?

Absolutely! Every enrolled student is assigned a dedicated career advisor. We coordinate profile reviews, mock interviews, and organize recruiter speed-dating events during the final two weeks of each program.

Are installment payment schedules available?

Yes. We offer zero-interest installment plans, allowing students to pay in four equal monthly installments. Contact our admissions staff for specialized scholarship requests.
